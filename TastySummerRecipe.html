Ingredients

  • 500g fresh strawberries
  • 2 lemons
  • 1 liter sparkling water
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Blend the strawberries into a smooth puree.
  2. Squeeze the lemons and add the juice.
  3. Stir in the honey until well combined.
  4. Pour in the sparkling water and mix gently.
  5. Serve over ice with a slice of lemon. Enjoy!
